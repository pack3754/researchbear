Meet Research Bear!

Research Bear says -

"If you want to succeed in life (and especially in your research projects), you're going to have to grow a pair."

Welcome to Research Bear, a website - for science!!!

If you're thinking about starting a research project, it's your lucky day! This site was created to provide students of the physical sciences with a set of tools, tutorials, and guidelines to aid in their research projects.

In the future, we hope to provide a wider range of resources to aid students in a variety of fields, but for now the rest of you are out of luck. Learn more details about the goals of Research Bear on the About page.
