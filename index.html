Meet Research Bear!

Research Bear says -

"If you want to succeed in your research, you're going to have to grow a pair."

Welcome to an "okay" website... for students!!! If you're thinking about starting a research project... well it's your lucky day! This site was created to provide UNCo physics students with a comprehensive set of tools, tutorials, and other resources to aid in their undergraduate research projects.

In the future, we hope to provide a wider range of resources to aid students in a variety of scientific fields. Learn more details on the About page.
