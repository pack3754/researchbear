Welcome to ResearchBear

Research Bear says -

"If you want to succeed, you're going to have to grow a pair."

This site is the product of a group of former physics students' effort to help up-and-coming scientists prepare for the wonderful world of research! This site is designed to provides UNCo physics students with a comprehensive set of resources to aid in their undergraduate research projects, with the hopes of encompassing all varieties of scientific research by students everywhere.

Ashamed Man says -
