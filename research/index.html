Research

This is the most important page on the entire site. Here you will find links to current and past student research projects. All of the projects listed below should conform with the project management guidelines described here. This means each project will have a web page that provides a synopsis and detailed description of the work, and an easily accessible link to the github repository where _ALL_ files for the project are organized and available for download. Having a standard format and guidelines for student projects is intended to make your work more accessible and transparent to professors and other students. Furthermore, it provides other students with a way of getting involved with your project.
